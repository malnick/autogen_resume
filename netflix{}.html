    Data Science and Engineering

    Senior Data Tools Engineer

    Data Science and Engineering

    Help us make modern business intelligence intuitive and friendly.

    Who are we?
    Netflix is growing fast and using the best new cloud technologies to ensure scalability and performance.  Our internal toolset is evolving rapidly.  These technologies don't always interact seamlessly, or have a decent (or any) front end. Complex, manual processes are often a waste of time and resources.  Help us find these situations and remedy them with friendly web interfaces, python or shell scripts, or just generally smarter workflows.  The resulting tools will be used across our department, and sometimes by our business users, so we want to emphasize usability.

    Who are you?
    - You see simple patterns where others see inscrutable noise.
    - You are lazy in a productive way (you find tedious work boring and would rather automate it).
    - You can translate from human to machine and back without losing the beauty and spirit of the intent.
    - You build people what they want before they know they wanted it.
    - You exhibit tenacity and thoroughness, even under pressure.
    - You have vision, and consider progress exciting.
    - You are known for turning work around quickly.
    - You can argue all sides of a debate convincingly, even if you harbor a preference.
    - You are motivated to explore new technologies and learn new things, while also recognizing that many satisfactory ideas and implementations may already exist.  You see engineering as a means, not an end.
    - You have been told that you are brilliant, even if you disagree.

    What do you know?
    - Web programming and design  
    - JavaScript, PHP, MySQL experience a plus
    - Cloud technology 
    - Python, Hadoop, MapReduce, Amazon EMR experience a plus
    - Reporting and visualization 
    - MicroStrategy experience a plus
    - Data modeling and SQL 
    - Teradata or other parallel database experience a plus
    - Education: surprise us 
    - A degree in Computer Science is not a requirement

    Don't know it?  Prove to us that you can learn it.
