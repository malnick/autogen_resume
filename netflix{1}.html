    Cloud Services Engineer

    Cloud and Platform Engineering

    Netflix is well-known in the industry as a leading exponent of Cloud Computing and one of the earliest large companies to migrate production systems to the cloud. We also actively contribute to the Open Source community wherein our architecture and design patterns have served as guidelines for AWS-based deployments.

    The Netflix Cloud Platform is at the heart of this, and builds the Platform-as-a-Service (PaaS) layer that all Netflix services and applications rely on. We are strong supporters of sharing useful libraries and ideas with the broader community and have open sourced many of the building blocks that comprise the Cloud Platform. Notable ones primarily owned by the team include Archaius, Eureka, Ribbon, Curator, Exhibitor and Governator with more to come. This is a golden opportunity to participate in the endeavor. For more of our work, check out: http://netflix.github.com.

    As Netflix expands internationally, the Cloud Platform team is pivotal to this growth plan. We are looking for an experienced, talented and motivated Cloud Services Engineer who will support our internal partners and highly distributed systems.

    Key Responsibilities
    - Troubleshoot production deployment related issues and help analyze Memory, Performance and general operational metrics
    - Interface with internal groups to whom we provide Platform Services and libraries
    - Implement end-to-end quality assessment and analytics for various distributed services
    - Participate in cross-functional projects and take ownership of individual feature efforts
    - Lead in the adoption of Continuous Delivery and automation of Platform services
    - Implement Tools and Dashboards for various services and operations in the Cloud Platform Infrastructure

    Minimum Job Qualifications
    - At least 3-5 years of relevant troubleshooting, integration and interfacing with cross-functional teams/projects.
    - Strong Java and object-oriented development skills
    - Experience  in JUnit, TestNG, Jenkins or similar systems
    - Experience with large-scale distributed systems and client-server architectures
    - Experience troubleshooting large scale, complex distributed systems
    - Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
    - Passion for understanding and analyzing complex systems behavior in the area of reliability, availability and performance 
    - BS/MS in computer science (or equivalent)
