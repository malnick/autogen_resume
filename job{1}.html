Senior Linux System Administrator

Full-Time - Redwood City, CA, United States
Tweet


Inflection, a fast-growing and profitable technology start-up in the Silicon Valley, is seeking a Senior Linux System Administrator who can script, pipe, package, and tune Inflection to new levels of success. The ideal candidate is hands-on, passionate about technology, and can sed, awk, and grep their way through any distro. This is a full-time position located in Redwood Shores.

You love it when there's a problem to solve

  • When there's a performance problem, you don't place blame, you hunt it down and tune it away
  • If you have a task you need to repeat often, you script it
  • If you can script it, you're also documenting it so others can support your work
  • You love to teach others on the team, so they can do it too
  • You're ready to solve problems at SCALE!
  • SANs make up the foundation for your virtual world and you are as comfortable there as any of your other Linux sandboxes.
  • Big Data excites you because it redefines "get it right the first time"
  • Clustering is fun because there are more instruments in your orchestra and you command them like the maestro that you are
  • You're so good at spinning up and deploying instances, that when someone asks you to spin, you only need to ask "How many?"  You are a true Puppet master
  • New application? New Service? No problem!  It's all technology to you and you're a technology athlete!
  • You're ready to join a guild of other IT professionals who share your passion

 
We're looking for a genuine PERL

  • BS in Computer Science or grok similar knowledge and understanding
  • 8+ years of experience working with Linux in a professional environment
  • Experience with multiple distributions including Ubuntu
  • Experience with enterprise Storage Area Networks (SANs) such as EMC, Hitachi or NetApp
  • Experience working with deployment tools such as Puppet or Chef
  • Strong background working with network service applications such as apache, postfix, mysql, memcached, and Redis.
  • Deep understanding of TCP/IP all the way from hardware-layer up to common services and routing principles
  • Ability to work collaboratively with teammates working in a Windows enterprise-scale data center
  • Strong focus on performance, quality, and scalability
  • Experience working with Cloud-based services and virtualization technologies such as Amazon Web Services and VMWare VCenter
